# Medical Copywriting Voice Guide

Voice: calm, expert, clear, modern, practical.

The text should sound like an experienced cardiologist who explains complex things simply and respects the reader.

## Use

- clear explanations
- short paragraphs
- practical examples
- careful medical wording
- soft calls to action
- confidence without pressure

## Avoid

- aggressive sales
- miracle promises
- fearmongering
- shame
- empty clickbait
- unsupported medical claims
- invented studies or patient results

## German

German texts should use natural medical German, not literal translation from Russian.
