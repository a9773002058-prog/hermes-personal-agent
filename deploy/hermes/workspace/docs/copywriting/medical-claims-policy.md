# Medical Claims Policy

Use conservative medical wording.

## Allowed

- educational explanations
- general prevention guidance
- lifestyle recommendations when framed as general information
- suggestions to consult a doctor for diagnosis or treatment
- cautious wording such as "may", "can be associated with", "often", "in many cases"

## Not Allowed

- guaranteed outcomes
- miracle claims
- cure claims without explicit verified context
- fake studies
- fake statistics
- fake patient stories
- diagnosis from incomplete user text
- universal advice for all patients

When details are missing, ask a clarifying question or add a medical caution note.
